Privacy Policy

At Flexe Shoe, your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

Information We Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, shipping address)
  • Payment details (processed securely through third-party payment providers)
  • Browsing data (pages visited, time spent, etc.)

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your Nike shoe orders
  • To send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • To improve our website and customer service
  • To send promotional emails (only if you subscribe)

How We Protect Your Data

We use secure servers, SSL encryption, and trusted payment gateways to protect your information. We do not store payment card details on our servers.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for payments, analytics, or marketing. These services only receive necessary data and are required to keep it confidential.

Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at: support@flexeshoe.com
